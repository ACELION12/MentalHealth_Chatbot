International & Selected Country Crisis Hotlines (short list)
- United States: National Suicide & Crisis Lifeline — Dial or text 988.
- India: Snehi / AASRA / EMPOWER helplines — check local listings for numbers (reach local emergency services if immediate danger).
- United Kingdom: Samaritans — 116 123.
- Australia: Lifeline — 13 11 14.
- Canada: Canada Suicide Prevention Service — 1.833.456.4566.
Note: If you are in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services first.
